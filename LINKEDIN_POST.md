## Alternative LinkedIn Article Version

**RapidReader: Accelerating Knowledge Intake Through RSVP Technology**

In our information-dense world, the ability to read quickly while maintaining comprehension is increasingly valuable. That's why I built RapidReader â€“ a web application that leverages the RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) technique to help users read faster and more efficiently.

**The Problem with Traditional Reading**

When we read traditionally, our eyes make constant movements (saccades) across lines of text and frequently re-fixate on words. These movements consume time and mental energy, limiting our reading speed and contributing to eye fatigue.

**The RSVP Solution**

RSVP eliminates these inefficiencies by presenting one word at a time in a fixed location. RapidReader enhances this with:

1. **Optimal Recognition Point (ORP)**: Each word has a "pivot letter" that's most efficient for recognition. RapidReader centers and emphasizes this point, helping your eyes lock onto the most important part of each word.

2. **Context Awareness**: Pure RSVP can feel disorienting. RapidReader displays a dimmed preview of surrounding words, maintaining sentence context and improving comprehension.

3. **Customizable Speed**: Adjustable WPM settings let you find your natural pace and gradually increase speed over time.

4. **Privacy-First Design**: All content and progress data stay in your browser â€“ nothing is sent to external servers.

**Technical Implementation**

Built with React, TypeScript, and Vite, RapidReader features:
- PDF text extraction for seamless document reading
- Three theme options (light, dark, sepia) for comfortable extended sessions
- LocalStorage-based persistence for progress tracking
- Clean, distraction-free interface focused on the reading experience

**Results**

Users can typically increase their reading speed significantly while maintaining or even improving comprehension, thanks to the reduced cognitive load from eliminated eye movements.
